5. Digital Evidence "DO'S & DON'TS"

The Golden Rule
"If it is ON, leave it ON.
If it is OFF, leave it OFF."

üì± On Seizure: Mobile Phones

  • Isolate: Airplane Mode + Wi-Fi OFF immediately.
  • Block Signals: Place in Faraday Bag. If unavailable, wrap in aluminum foil (min 3 layers).
  • Power: Do not rely on "Lock Screen"; keep device powered if unlocked.

üíª Computers/Laptops

If OFF: Do not turn it on to "check."

If ON: Photograph screen. Pull power plug (desktops) to bypass shutdown sequences.

Volatile Data: If forensic expert present, capture RAM.

üì° Routers & Modems

Often ignored, but crucial. They contain logs of connected devices. Seize them if relevant.
